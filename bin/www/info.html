Data for MGV have been obtained from the following resources:
  • Genome assemblies for 19 mouse strains from ENSEMBL.
  • Gene models for 18 mouse strains from ENSEMBL.
  • Gene models for C57BL/6J from MGI.
  • Genome assemblies for non-mouse genomes from NCBI.
  • Gene models for non-mouse genomes from the Alliance for Genome Resources.
  • Orthology assertions from the Alliance for Genome Resources.
